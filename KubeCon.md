New to CNCF? 5 Practical Tips to Get Involved and Make Your First Contribution

Getting started with CNCF projects can feel intimidating. Big codebases, complex systems, and experienced maintainers often make new contributors feel like they need to “learn everything first” before sending their first PR.

In this lightning talk, I’ll share 5 simple, practical tips that helped me go from a small first contribution to a CNCF incubating project in my first year of college to becoming a CKA, getting selected for the prestigious LFX mentorship, speaking at global conferences like KubeCon, and eventually landing an SRE role.

I’ll also share common mistakes to avoid and how to navigate the projects when things feel overwhelming. If you’re just getting started with CNCF, no matter your experience level, this session will help you understand how to get involved in the CNCF ecosystem.
